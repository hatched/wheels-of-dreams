How you can help!

Putting together a great team and corporate sponsors are only the first step - we still need your help to reach our goals. 100% of the money donated to Wheels Of Dreams goes directly to the Childrens Hospital Foundation. In fact, when clicking the donate button below you will be taken directly to the Childrens Hospital Foundation donation form.

We will be giving away prizes at random to awesome people like yourself who donate to the Wheels Of Dreams project. To get yourself added into the draws simply add Wheels Of Dreams into the comment box on the donation form.

Thank you for your support!
